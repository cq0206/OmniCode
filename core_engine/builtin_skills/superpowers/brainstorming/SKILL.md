# Brainstorming

Turn a rough request into a concrete design before editing code.

## Focus

- Understand the real goal of the request.
- Capture constraints from the workspace, tape history, and project rules.
- Consider multiple approaches before choosing a direction.
- Keep the design scoped to the smallest useful slice.

## Deliverable

Produce a design artifact that explains:

- What problem is being solved.
- Which approach is recommended and why.
- How the work should be partitioned.
- What testing and risk areas matter.
